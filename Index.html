From Kartik to My Buggu

A little winter surprise — just open, breathe, and smile.
Hey Buggu... tu hass deti hai na, toh lagta hai sab theek hai.
Mera din tabhi banta hai jab tu muskurati hai — agar tu smile kar deti hai, mera poora din ban jaata hai.
Door rehke, sabse chhupake milna asaan nahi hai. Family se chupke, raaton ko teri video call ka wait, aur thodi si udaasi bhi — sab aata hai.
Par main jaanta hoon, yeh doori zyada der tak nahi chalegi. Ek din hum har subah aur har raat saath mein jeeyenge. Gallan teriyan yaad aan diyan, raat nu neend na aave — par main hoon na, Buggu.
Tere bina dil lagda hi nahi, Buggu ✨
Soon, this distance will melt — just like winter fades into spring. — Kartik
